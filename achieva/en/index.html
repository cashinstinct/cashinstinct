Deposit Security

Unlimited DGCM Coverage vs CDIC: Why It Matters

This is Achieva Financial's most underrated advantage. Most Canadian banks are covered by the CDIC (Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation), which caps protection at $100,000 per deposit category, per institution. Anything above that ceiling is no longer insured.

Achieva, as a division of Cambrian Credit Union (Manitoba), is instead covered by the Deposit Guarantee Corporation of Manitoba (DGCM), which guarantees 100% of deposits with no cap whatsoever. A $250,000 emergency fund or a maxed-out TFSA held over several years is fully protected — something that's impossible at a CDIC institution without splitting your accounts.
