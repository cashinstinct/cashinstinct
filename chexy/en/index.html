Referral link or promo code: what's the difference?

These are two distinct mechanics, with different conditions. The referral link gives the $20 welcome bonus: it attaches at account creation (no code to enter) and unlocks as soon as your first payment is completed — recurring or one-time. The promo code is a discount applied at the payment step, and only on a recurring payment: it doesn't work on a one-time payment (like a single tax payment), nor retroactively on a payment already completed or in progress. In practice: use the link for the $20, and if you also have a promo code, save it for a recurring payment.

When is your Chexy credit issued, and how do you use it?

Your credit (referral bonus or Chexy Cash) is added after a payment has been successfully completed: bill payment sent, Interac transfer deposited, pre-authorized debit (PAD) withdrawn, or EFT processed. To use it, you redeem it on your next payment: in the "Wallet" for an existing payment, or directly at the checkout screen for a new one. Important detail: redeem your credit before your card is charged — that's about 4 days before the due date for an existing payment. The credit applies to both recurring and one-time payments, but cannot be withdrawn or transferred: it only serves to reduce your payments through Chexy.

Is the $20 bonus a transfer or a credit?

It's a Chexy credit, not a transfer to your bank account. Once your first payment is completed, the $20 appears as a credit balance in your Chexy account. You then redeem it via the "Redeem" button (in the Wallet or at the checkout screen) to reduce a future payment made through Chexy. Two concrete consequences: the credit applies to an active recurring payment, and it cannot be withdrawn or transferred — it only serves to lower the cost of a Chexy payment (for example, wiping out part of the 1.75% fee).
