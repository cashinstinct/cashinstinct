Digital proof (BAT) A preview of your design that Sticker Mule sends before production begins. You can request unlimited free revisions — no extra fees — before approving. Production only starts after your approval. BAT stands for "Bon À Tirer" (French for "good to print").
Die cut sticker A sticker cut precisely to the outline of your design, with no square or rectangular border. The #1 most ordered product on Sticker Mule with over 6.3 million orders. Includes a protective laminate — weatherproof, UV-resistant, and dishwasher-safe.

Window cling A sticker that clings to glass without adhesive — repositionable and removable without residue. Currently the most searched product on Sticker Mule. No protective laminate; not designed for outdoor weather exposure.
Front adhesive sticker A sticker applied from the front with the design visible through a transparent surface (e.g., inside a window, facing outward). No laminate — not designed for outdoor or dishwasher use.
Holographic sticker A sticker with a reflective, rainbow-effect foil background. The #5 most ordered product on Sticker Mule with over 900,000 orders. Includes protective laminate — weatherproof and dishwasher-safe.
