Frequently Asked Questions — HP Instant Ink

How do I get the free month with code SVdn7?

Enter referral code SVdn7 at the billing step of the HP account sign-up form, just before confirming your chosen plan. The system will then credit a compensatory balance equivalent to one full billing cycle.

Does the code work if I already have free months included with my printer purchase?

Yes, absolutely — that's the stacking principle. If your new printer comes with 6 free months of HP+, entering code SVdn7 during registration will add a 7th free month right after the others.

Is the service available for laser printers?

Yes, HP Instant Ink offers specific plans for toner cartridges used in laser printers. The logic remains the same: automatic replenishment happens before the reservoirs are empty.

Can I cancel the subscription easily?

Yes, the service has no fixed-term commitment. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your profile, or pause the subscription if you no longer need automated ink.

Do we own the ink cartridges provided?

No. Instant Ink cartridges remain the exclusive property of HP. They are loaned as part of your subscription. If you cancel the service, these specific cartridges stop working electronically and must be returned to HP via the provided recycling envelope.

Is a permanent Wi-Fi connection required?

Yes. For the service to work, your printer must remain connected to the Internet via your home Wi-Fi network. This is what allows HP's servers to algorithmically track the number of pages printed and remaining ink level in order to trigger automatic shipments.

How does the rollover of unused pages work?

If you print fewer pages than your monthly plan quota, the remaining pages are not lost. They automatically roll over to the following month. The maximum rollover balance depends on your plan (generally up to two or three times your monthly quota).

What happens if I exceed my monthly plan?

If you exceed your monthly page quota (and have no rolled-over pages in reserve), HP applies a small charge for an additional page block (for example, $1.25 for a block of 10 to 50 pages depending on the plan). Everything is handled automatically on your next invoice.

Does printing colour photos use more of my plan?

No — and that is HP Instant Ink's main advantage. A page is a page. Whether you print a single line of black and white text or a full-colour high-definition photo on letter-size paper, it counts as exactly 1 page against your plan.

Can I change my plan up or down mid-subscription?

Yes, the service is fully flexible. You can change your plan at any time from your online dashboard. An upgrade may apply immediately or at the next cycle, while a downgrade will take effect at your next billing period.

Does referral code SVdn7 work for HP Instant Ink in France and Europe?

Yes. The referral link and code SVdn7 are universal. HP's system automatically adapts the free month value and local currency (Euros) based on the geographic region detected during printer registration.

What is the difference between an HP promotional code and a referral code?

HP promo codes are temporary advertising keywords tied to marketing events. A referral code (here SVdn7) is a permanent member identifier that applies a guaranteed one-free-month trial offer with no risk of expiration.
